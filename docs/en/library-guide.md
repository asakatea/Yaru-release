# Library

The Library is your personal reading space. It stores books, articles, and documents in one place and gives you AI-powered tools to get more out of everything you read.

## Collection Management

Books and documents are organized into **collections**. Filter by category, author, format, or reading status (unread, in progress, finished). Drag items between collections or use bulk actions to reorganize quickly.

## Markdown Book Reader

Open any Markdown-based book to read it in a clean, distraction-free layout. The reader supports a full table of contents sidebar, adjustable font size, and both light and dark themes. Internal links and wiki-style references are tappable.

## PDF Reader

Import PDF files into your library for a native reading experience. The reader supports page navigation, zoom, text selection, and annotation. Bookmarks let you jump back to important pages instantly.

## AI Intel Toolbar

While reading any document, open the **AI Intel** toolbar for on-demand intelligence:

- **Translate** -- Select a passage and translate it into your preferred language.
- **Mind Map** -- Generate a visual mind map from the current chapter or selection.
- **Quiz** -- Create a quick comprehension quiz based on what you just read.
- **Flashcard Generation** -- Turn key facts into Memory flashcards with one tap.

All AI features run through Yaru's secure gateway. Your reading data stays private.

## Bookmarks and Reading Progress

Tap the bookmark icon on any page to save your spot. The library home screen shows a progress bar for each item so you can see at a glance how far along you are. Resume reading from exactly where you stopped.

## YAR Packages

YAR is Yaru's content distribution format. A `.yar` package bundles a book or resource with metadata, cover art, and optional flashcard decks into a single portable file. Download YAR packages from the community market or create your own in the Studio to share with others.
