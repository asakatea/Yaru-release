# Collaboration Guide

## Audience and scope
This guide covers social and team collaboration paths in Yaru.

## Core capabilities
- Forum threads and replies
- Team spaces and role-based membership
- Collaborative content review and communication loops

## Typical workflow
1. Open or join a topic thread.
2. Share resources and structured feedback.
3. Use team roles for scoped ownership.
4. Track follow-up actions in task-oriented modules.

## Practical tips
- Keep thread titles specific and searchable.
- Separate question, solution, and review phases.
- Use role permissions to avoid accidental edits.

## Troubleshooting
- Cannot post/reply: verify authentication and role.
- Missing team actions: check membership and role assignments.
- Inconsistent visibility: confirm workspace scope/public flags.
