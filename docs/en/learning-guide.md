# Learning

The Learning module is your structured study hub. It combines step-based courses, productivity tools, and deep-learning resources into a single dashboard so you can focus on making progress every day.

## Dashboard Overview

When you open Learning, the dashboard shows three things at a glance:

- **Daily progress** -- a summary of completed steps, study time, and streaks.
- **Enrolled courses** -- cards for every course you have joined, with a progress bar and a "Continue" button.
- **Recommendations** -- suggested courses and resources based on your activity.

If you have not enrolled in anything yet, the dashboard displays a friendly empty state with quick links to browse the catalog.

## Enrolled Courses and Step-Based Lessons

Each course is broken into **lessons**, and each lesson is a sequence of **steps**. Steps come in four types:

| Step | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| **Explain** | Introduces a concept with text, diagrams, or media. |
| **Example** | Walks through a concrete, worked example. |
| **Practice** | Tests your understanding with interactive exercises. |
| **Summary** | Recaps key takeaways before you move on. |

Complete all steps in a lesson to unlock the next one. Your progress is saved automatically, so you can pick up exactly where you left off.

## Productivity Tools

Two built-in tools sit alongside your courses:

- **Todo List** -- Create, check off, and prioritize tasks related to your studies. Overdue items surface at the top.
- **Pomodoro Timer** -- A configurable focus timer (default 25 min work / 5 min break). Start it from any course page to track focused study sessions.

## Deep Learning Resources

Below your enrolled courses, the dashboard offers curated deep-learning sections:

- **Wiki** -- A searchable reference for formulas, theorems, and definitions.
- **Courses Catalog** -- Browse and enroll in new courses across multiple disciplines.
- **AI Exercises** -- Practice problems generated or guided by AI, tailored to the topics you are studying.

## Course Progress Tracking

Open any enrolled course to see a detailed progress view: completed steps, remaining steps, estimated time to finish, and a visual timeline of your study sessions. Use this view to plan your study schedule and stay on track.
